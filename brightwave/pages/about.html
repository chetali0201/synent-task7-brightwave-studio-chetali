Our Story

About BrightWave Studio

Three people who got tired of watching small businesses get ignored online.

How it started

We started in a tiny shared office in 2023.

Honestly, it wasn't glamorous. Three of us — a developer, a designer, and a marketer — crammed into a co-working space in Bandra with one shared monitor and way too many strong teas.

We kept seeing the same problem: good local businesses with terrible (or no) online presence, getting overlooked because they couldn't afford big agencies or didn't know where to start. So we decided to just... help them.

Three years later, we've worked with over 40 clients across Mumbai, Pune, and Bengaluru. We've grown a little, our office got slightly bigger, and we now have a proper coffee machine.

What we stand for

Our Values

Not just stuff we put on a slide deck — things we actually try to practice.

🤝

Honest communication

We'll tell you if something won't work, even if it means losing a project. No false promises about timelines or overnight results.

🔍

Details matter

Pixel-perfect isn't always the goal, but caring about the small things — load times, accessibility, mobile layouts — always is.

💡

Keep it simple

The best solution is usually the simplest one. We're not fans of overcomplicating things just to look impressive.

📈

Results over aesthetics

A beautiful website that gets zero traffic is a failure. We design with purpose — to help your business actually grow.
